🍽️ About Demo Restaurant

Demo Restaurant – a beloved dining spot in Mangaldoi, Assam. Known for clean, quality food at reasonable prices with friendly staff. We serve North Indian, Chinese, Rolls, Momos, and street food delights. Perfect for family outings, quick bites, or no-contact delivery.

Dine-in · Takeaway · Delivery only (no seating available for delivery orders)

Open for breakfast, lunch, dinner. Average cost ₹200 for one.

Popular: Chicken Pulao, Kadai Paneer, Chicken Roll, Momos.
